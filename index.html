Aaron Badilla

Actor, Writer, Musician

Aaron is an actor, writer, and musician based out of Brooklyn, NY. His recent projects include writing and directing The Fling LP at New York Theater Summerfest, starring in the indie/comedy short, Self Servers, and the pop/rock single "Blue Light" which he released with his band LoopBack.
